1 Brief description¶

SlicedEigenEnergies are the one-electron Hartree–Fock energies \(\varepsilon_p\), partinioned into hole states \(i\) and particle states \(a\), where \(\varepsilon_i<\varepsilon_\mathrm{F}\) and \(\varepsilon_\mathrm{F}<\varepsilon_a\). The Fermi energy \(\varepsilon_\mathrm{F}\) is specified in the metaData section of the EigenEnergies. This partitioning is done by the DefineHolesAndParticles algorithm.
