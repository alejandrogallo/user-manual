Contents

  • Contact

The code is mainly developed in the group of Andreas Grüneis at the Technical University Vienna https://cqc.itp.tuwien.ac.at/.

If you have questions about installing or applying cc4s, you can open an issue on gitlab https://gitlab.cc4s.html/cc4s/cc4s/-/issues. You can also write an email to any of the developers, but we would recommend the use of gitlab.
